Journey from "Bindu se Sindhu"

In response to a nationwide survey revealing that nearly half of Indian homemakers aspire to start their own businesses..., we were motivated to create a platform specifically tailored for these entrepreneurial-minded women. The survey conducted by Nielsen highlighted the challenges faced by homemakers, with 69% citing insufficient funds and 63% pointing to household responsibilities as significant roadblocks to realizing their entrepreneurial dreams. Inspired by the findings of the Marie Gold Indian Women Entrepreneurship Survey Report 2018, where 54% expressed the desire to create their own identity through business, our platform aims to provide a supportive space for homemakers to pursue their entrepreneurial goals. Recognizing the prevalent aspirations of homemakers to see their daughters run their own businesses, we established this platform as a means to foster economic independence and empowerment among women. With insights into the common business ventures homemakers aspire to undertake, such as tailoring, salons, boutiques, and home tuition, our platform strives to address the specific needs and aspirations of this demographic, offering tailored solutions for a successful entrepreneurial journey.
